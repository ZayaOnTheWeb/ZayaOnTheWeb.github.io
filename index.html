About me

Hi, nice to meet ya, I'm Zaya. Through my work as a UX-Designer, I love helping people by employing a sprinkle of technology, a handful of creativity, and a lot of curiosity to create inspiring experiences and helpful solutions.

In my university's honours programme, I learned how to apply design thinking in various settings by leading creative workshops to redesign The Hague. I also am experienced in using UX-Design in healthcare.

Within my one-and-a-half years of studying UX-Design, I've learned to use various tools such as Figma, Protopie, Miro, VS Code, and I am looking forward to learning a lot more!

I am a versatile designer and like to stretch the boundaries of where Design Thinking can be applied.
